About

I am a master's student in Agricultural Economics at McGill University. My research interests lie in sustainability, energy policy, and agricultural development, with a focus on understanding how changes in the energy mix affect economic and environmental outcomes in border economies.

Research

  • Pulse Supply Chain Sustainability in Quebec: Evaluating greenhouse gas reduction potential through expanded pulse adoption scenarios.
  • Energy Poverty and Rural Development in Kenya: Applied the Multidimensional Energy Poverty Index (MEPI) to analyze electricity access inequality.

Publications & Presentations

  • “Carbon Reduction and Profitability in Quebec Pulse Supply Chains.” Canadian Economics Association Annual Conference, 2024.
  • “Rural Electrification and Multidimensional Energy Poverty: Evidence from Kenya.” Development Economics Workshop, 2023.
